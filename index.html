About the Author

MARVELLOUS N. GERALD is a passionate storyteller, book writer, and creative thinker whose words inspire, entertain, and transform. With a deep love for literature and human connection, she writes to touch hearts, spark imaginations, and leave lasting impressions.

Her works explore themes of love, resilience, and the human spirit, making her a unique voice in contemporary writing. When she's not writing, Marvellous enjoys connecting with readers, sharing insights on storytelling, and helping others discover the power of words.
